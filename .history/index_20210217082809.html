A neighbourhood Café

Jing is Albany’s new neighbourhood cafe and eatery. An homage to Albany’s roots as a fruit growing district coupled with a modern and diverse food offering to reflect where we are today.  
We’re inspired by all cultures and cuisines but determined by none. We use real ingredients and focus on simple, delicious flavours that cater to all tastes and ages.

Only Fresh Food

Tracing all the way back to the 1890s, a lot of Aucklanders would travel North to the fruit-growing district of Albany to get their fresh apples, strawberries, pears and peaches. Fields inherits this purpose and aims to create a café where fresh is best.
