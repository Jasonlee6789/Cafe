Booking

We kindly take bookings for 4 and above.

We welcome small walk-ins groups of less than 4 people during our trading hours and run a very efficient waitlist system.

For request for 12 people or more, special events or private dining room, please contact us directly on 09-4145888 or hello@fieldscafe.co.nz. You could also leave a message for us in the EVENTS page and we will get back to you ASAP.

For today's availability, you could also contact us through 09-4145888.
